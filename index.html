The honest version

Why Customer Success?

When I think about why I'm drawn to Customer Success, one thing stands out clearly: it is one of the few roles where being human is your greatest strength , and AI actually makes that more true, not less. There's a lot of conversation around AI replacing Customer Success roles. But here's my take: AI can empathize in words, but it cannot sit across from a CISO who's frustrated at 11 PM before a board meeting and genuinely make them feel heard. It cannot pick up on the hesitation in a customer's voice or adjust its approach based on a subtle shift in the room. That authentic, human advocacy, that's what builds trust at the highest levels.

What excites me about Customer Success today is the chance to operate at the intersection of human connection and intelligent tools. I want to use AI to take the redundant work off my plate (the reporting, the follow-ups, the noise) so I can pour my focus into truly understanding what keeps my customers up at night and championing solutions for them.

In a world where every company is deploying AI, the CSM who can combine emotional intelligence with AI fluency will be the one customers fight to keep. That's the version of this role I want to build toward — and that's why Customer Success.

— JUHI SHARMA
